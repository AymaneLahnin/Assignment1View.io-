Our Menu

Chicken

Indulge in a world of flavor with our Chicken Menu. From the **Savory Chicken Delight** featuring succulent grilled chicken breast adorned with aromatic herbs and spices, to the crispy perfection of our **Crispy Buttermilk Chicken** served alongside velvety mashed potatoes and country-style gravy. For a lighter option, savor the **Zesty Citrus Chicken Salad**, where tender grilled chicken harmonizes with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and candied pecans, all drizzled with a refreshing citrus vinaigrette. Each dish is a culinary masterpiece, ensuring a delightful experience that captures the essence of succulent chicken in every bite.

Sushi

Embark on a culinary journey with our Sushi Menu, where traditional meets innovation. Our Sushi Fusion Platter presents a captivating blend of classic and contemporary sushi rolls, delivering a symphony of flavors in each bite. For the purists, the Sashimi Symphony showcases the elegance of raw fish, perfectly complemented by wasabi and soy reduction. Seeking a plant-based delight? Our Vegetarian Maki Magic is a celebration of vibrant veggies and creamy avocado, kissed by the rich umami of miso glaze. Elevate your senses with sushi that transcends tradition.

Beef

Welcome to a world of beefy indulgence on our Beef Menu. The Grilled Prime Steak commands attention with its char-grilled perfection, partnered with truffle-infused mashed potatoes and a sumptuous red wine reduction. Craving a classic? Our Burger Bliss takes center stage, featuring a juicy beef patty, melted aged cheddar, and our secret sauce, all embraced by a freshly baked brioche bun. Seeking adventure? Dive into the Korean BBQ Beef Tacos, where the bold flavors of beef bulgogi mingle with spicy gochujang sauce and zesty kimchi, wrapped in warm corn tortillas. Savor beef at its finest.
